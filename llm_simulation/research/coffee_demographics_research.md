# Coffee Shop Consumer Behaviour — Demographics Research
*Compiled from NCA, Toast POS, Mintel, Grand View Research, Euromonitor, Statista, Coffee Intelligence, ResearchGate (2024–2025)*

---

## By Age Group

### Gen Z (18–25)
- **Daily consumption:** 42% (lowest of all generations)
- **Monthly visits:** 8–10
- **Drink preference:** Cold/iced/frozen (57%); only 43% prefer hot coffee
- **Specialty coffee past-day:** 46%
- **Market revenue share:** 31.9% of specialty coffee market (2024)
- **Customisation:** 45% place high value on personalisation and aesthetic drinks
- **Mobile ordering:** 73% have used in past 3 months
- **Price sensitivity:** High (7–8% price increases show significant demand reduction)
- **Loyalty type:** Exploration-driven; try new venues and drinks frequently
- **Venue preference:** Visually appealing, Instagram-worthy; social-media-friendly spaces
- **Decision drivers:** Taste + aesthetics, customisation, social media discoverability
- **Time pattern:** 40–50% of visits after 2 PM (afternoon/evening focus)
- **Dwell time:** 45–60 min (use cafés as study/social spaces)
- **Key insight:** Highest spending power ($360bn disposable income) despite lower daily frequency

### Young Millennials (26–33)
- **Daily consumption:** 62–66%
- **Specialty engagement:** 64% consume specialty coffee weekly (highest of any group)
- **Market CAGR:** 10.3% growth 2025–2030 (fastest growing segment)
- **Drink preference:** Specialty drinks, pour-overs, milk-based; cold beverage shift (60% still prefer hot)
- **Price sensitivity:** Moderate–low; willing to pay premium for quality and sustainability
- **Loyalty type:** Balanced — explores specialty cafés but builds loyalties to favourites
- **Venue preference:** Aesthetic appeal, sustainability credentials, specialty cafés
- **Decision drivers:** Quality, sustainability/ethics (65% pay more for sustainable sourcing), curiosity
- **Time pattern:** Balanced morning and afternoon; remote work extends afternoon café use
- **Key insight:** Primary driver of specialty coffee growth globally

### Senior Millennials (34–40)
- Same as Young Millennials but higher loyalty concentration; more likely to have a "regular"
- Remote work impact: extended café sessions 2–3×/week for work-from-home workers
- Higher average transaction values (~$7–10/visit)
- More selective; fewer venues but deeper loyalty to chosen spots

### Gen X (41–56)
- **Daily consumption:** 74%
- **Monthly visits:** 10–12
- **Self-identify as coffee aficionados:** 41% (YouGov)
- **Drink preference:** Traditional quality coffees; pour-overs, quality drip
- **Price sensitivity:** Moderate; value consistency and reasonable pricing
- **Loyalty type:** Loyalty-driven; established routines; rarely explores new venues
- **Venue preference:** Reliable spots near commute or office; consistent quality
- **Decision drivers:** Taste, convenience, consistency
- **Time pattern:** Strong weekday morning preference; weekend brunch

### Boomers (57+)
- **Daily consumption:** 83–85% (highest of all generations)
- **Monthly visits:** 6–9
- **Specialty adoption:** Lowest (43% past-day specialty)
- **Drink preference:** 89% prefer hot drip coffee
- **Average transaction:** $9–13 (highest spend per visit)
- **Price sensitivity:** Moderate; prefer affordability for routine purchases
- **Loyalty type:** Extremely high loyalty; rarely switches venues
- **Venue preference:** Familiar, accessible, comfortable; conviviality and routine
- **Decision drivers:** Convenience, price, consistency, brand trust
- **Time pattern:** Weekday mornings dominant; weekend brunch

## By Occupation

### Office / Corporate Workers
- **Visit frequency:** 3–5×/week during workdays (12–20 visits/month)
- **Market share:** 42% of total coffee shop customers
- **Peak times:** 7–9 AM commute; 2–4 PM afternoon break
- **Decision drivers:** Location convenience, speed, reliability, WiFi
- **Loyalty type:** Very high (routine-driven; same 2–3 spots near office)
- **Venue preference:** Chains near office buildings and commuter routes
- **Spending:** Regular AM/PM visits; predictable spend

### Healthcare Workers
- **Visit frequency:** 12–16 visits/month (above average)
- **Driver:** Demanding schedules; coffee culture embedded in profession
- **Time pattern:** Variable shift-driven; throughout the day including nights
- **Decision drivers:** Efficiency, speed of service, proximity to hospital
- **Loyalty type:** Moderate; convenience-driven, less exploratory

### Students / Academic
- **Visit frequency:** 8–10/month; highly variable
- **Time pattern:** Afternoon-heavy (40–50% after 2 PM)
- **Dwell time:** 45–60 min average (longest of any group)
- **Infrastructure needs:** WiFi, quiet space, power outlets, comfortable seating
- **Decision drivers:** Atmosphere (study-friendly), price, WiFi quality
- **Price sensitivity:** Moderate–high; price-aware but experience-driven
- **Loyalty type:** Mixed; exploration within budget; loyal to study-friendly spots

### Remote Workers / Digital Nomads
- **Visit frequency:** 15–20+/month (highest of any group)
- **60%** prefer coffee shops when not at home (vs. 40% co-working spaces)
- **Time pattern:** Late morning sessions + afternoon work blocks; extended stays (2+ hrs)
- **Infrastructure needs:** WiFi speed, power outlets, vacancy, moderate noise, bathroom access
- **Spending:** Higher per-visit than average; 75% eat lunch out ≥1×/week
- **Loyalty type:** Location-loyal (same spots that meet infrastructure needs)
- **Decision drivers:** WiFi reliability, space availability, ambience for productivity

### Trade / Manual Workers
- **Visit frequency:** 5–8/month; morning-heavy
- **Time pattern:** Very early morning (5:30–7:30 AM before site start)
- **Decision drivers:** Speed, price, proximity to job site
- **Drink preference:** Functional; hot drip, black coffee, strong tea
- **Venue preference:** Drive-through chains; grab-and-go
- **Price sensitivity:** High; value-driven

### Executives / Senior Managers
- **Visit frequency:** 8–12/month
- **Venue preference:** Quality independents; client-facing premium cafés
- **Decision drivers:** Quality, atmosphere for informal meetings, brand prestige
- **Price sensitivity:** Low; expense-account culture
- **Loyalty type:** High loyalty to premium venues; client entertainment lens

### Creative / Media / Design
- **Visit frequency:** 12–16/month (similar to healthcare; café = second office)
- **Venue preference:** Independent, aesthetic, character-filled cafés
- **Decision drivers:** Atmosphere, aesthetic, quality, neighbourhood
- **Dwell time:** Long; treat café as creative workspace
- **Loyalty type:** Neighbourhood-loyal; explores within local area
